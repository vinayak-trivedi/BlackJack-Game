Rules of game

  • To win the game you need need a BlackJack i.e. the sum of all your cards should be equal to 21.
  • If the sum of all your cards is less than 21 then you are allowed to draw another card.
  • If the sum of all your cards is greater than 21 then you are out of the game. But no worries! you can restart the game and play again by clicking on the start game button.
  • Every time you hit the start game button 10 chips are deducted from the pool of chips you have, and 5 chips are deducted when you click on new card button.
